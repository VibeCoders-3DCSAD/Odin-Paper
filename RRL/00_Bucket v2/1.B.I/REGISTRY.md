Title: Profiling Platform Workers in the Philippines: Evidence from the Jobs and Skills Survey

Author/s: Jandoc et al.

Description:
Examines non-traditional platform work, including gig labor, short-term contracting, part-time work, home-based work, and online freelancing, as new avenues for flexibility and income diversification for Filipinos.
- Explains Income Sources: Notes that the rise of flexible and non-traditional jobs is a response to the search for new income sources amid limited formal employment opportunities, reinforcing certain employment patterns.
- Discusses Income Instability: Highlights the vulnerability of gig and freelance workers to income shocks and job instability due to the lack of written contracts, paid leave, social insurance, or unemployment protection.
