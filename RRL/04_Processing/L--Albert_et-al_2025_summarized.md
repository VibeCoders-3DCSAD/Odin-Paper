

---
Utilizes quantitative data from multiple waves of national surveys, including the Labor Force Survey (LFS) and the Family Income and Expenditure Survey (FIES), to examine employment patterns and economic information for working-age adults (15-64 years), which includes the target demographic of young professionals.
- Includes Formal Employment Data: Incorporates data from major administrative sources, such as the Government Service Insurance System (GSIS) and Social Security System (SSS), to provide insights into formal sector employment and social protection access among different population groups.
- Examines PSA Data Sources: Draws on data from the Philippine Statistics Authority (PSA), specifically the SDG Watch Database, Annual Poverty Indicators Survey (APIS), and the Family Income and Expenditure Survey (FIES), which are the requested institutional sources for population and household economic data.
