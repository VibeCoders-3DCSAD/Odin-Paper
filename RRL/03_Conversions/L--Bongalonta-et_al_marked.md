This study concludes that the faculty members of SorSU Bulan
Campus adopt both the traditional way of saving money and the mod-
ern style of investment as modes of saving their finances which in-
ing bank accounts and deposits and purchasing investment instru-
clude budgeting, joining in “paluwagan”, using piggy bank, maintain-
ments. Increasing cost of utilities and unexpected day-to-day ex-
penses; lack of financial literacy; poor debt management; and low and
delayed salaries were the problems encountered by the faculty par-
ticipants in handling their monetary resources. From the results of the
study, this highly recommends the adoption of the Bongalonta’s Fi-
nancial Model for SorSU Bulan Campus Faculty Members de-
signed by the researcher as a guide or model for saving money. It is
likewise recommended that seminars and trainings on financial liter-
acy may be conducted.
